ABOUT THIS TOOL

Built for Accuracy.
Designed for Everyone.

The Age Difference Calculator is a free, browser-based tool that computes the precise age gap between two people based on their dates of birth. It accounts for leap years, variable month lengths, and provides a complete breakdown of the difference.

All calculations happen entirely in your browser. No data is ever transmitted to a server, ensuring your complete privacy and allowing it to work even without an internet connection.
